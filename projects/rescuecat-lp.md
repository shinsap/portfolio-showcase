# Rescue Cat Card LP

## Overview

Rescue Cat Card LP is the public-facing landing page for Rescue Cat Card. It explains the service concept, use case, and path to registration for people who want emergency pet-care information to be reachable when something happens to them.

## Challenge → Solution

**Problem:** Rescue Cat Card solves a real problem, but the value is not obvious at a glance — "what happens to my cat if I collapse?" is not something people think about until it's too late. The LP needs to make that scenario feel real and immediate, then reduce hesitation to register.

**Solution:** A story-driven landing page that opens with the worst-case scenario, explains what the card does and how scanning works, and connects directly to the Stripe purchase flow. Designed to convert visitors who arrive from search, social sharing, or word-of-mouth with no prior knowledge of the service.

- Astro

- Cloudflare Pages
- Stripe Payment Link

## Relationship to Rescue Cat Card v4

Rescue Cat Card v4 is the launched service application with authentication, user records, QR/card-related flows, Supabase, and Cloudflare-based operation.

Rescue Cat Card LP is the acquisition layer. It presents the service to new users and creates a clean path toward the application via Stripe checkout.

## What This Demonstrates

- Story-driven landing page design for an abstract-value service
- Conversion-oriented page structure with Stripe integration
- Separation between product application and marketing surface
- Static site deployment on Cloudflare Pages
